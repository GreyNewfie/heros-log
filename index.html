Welcome to Hero's Log

This app allows HeroQuest players to track their Hero's progress through the use of a digital character sheet.

The equipped items section will allow you to track which items your character is currently using.

Turn on the "Auto Update Stats" option to have your character's maximum body and mind points automatically updated when items with modifiers are equipped.

To begin select "Create a Character".
