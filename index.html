Welcome to Hero's Log

This app allows HeroQuest players to track their Hero's progress through the use of a digital character sheet.

The character outline tracks which items your character is currently using. Any item cards that have the "equip" option can be equipped. Equipped item images are clickable to display their item card.

Turn on "Automatically Update Stats" to have your character's attack & defend dice, starting body & mind points updated as items with modifiers are equipped. Enable this feature before equipping items.

To begin select "Create a Character".
